```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<dialogues>
		<dialogue>
			<order>1</order>
			<speaker>Player</speaker>
			<text>Hello, what's your story?</text>
		</dialogue>
		<dialogue>
			<order>2</order>
			<npc_name>NPC</npc_name>
			<text>I'm just a simple NPC. What can I do for you?</text>
		</dialogue>
	</dialogues>
</game>
```
The original XML data was missing the closing `>` for the `<dialogues>` tag and each `<dialogue>` tag should have a unique `order` attribute. The corrected version includes these changes and also adds example values for the dialogue order, speaker/npc_name, and text.
