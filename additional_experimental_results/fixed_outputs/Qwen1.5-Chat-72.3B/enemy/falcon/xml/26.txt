The original XML is missing the opening `<game>` tag and has an extra closing `</stats>` tag. Here's the corrected XML with a new enemy example:

```xml
<?xml version="1.0" ?>
<game>
	<enemy>
		<id>12345</id>
		<name>BossDragon</name>
		<description>A fierce dragon that guards the treasure.</description>
		<stats>
			<hp>200</hp>
			<mp>50</mp>
			<atk>30</atk>
			<def>20</def>
			<spd>15</spd>
		</stats>
	</enemy>
</game>
```
