**Example usage**: To use your character profile, include the following code in a post or a page.

```xml
<game>
	<character>
		<id>1</id>
		<first-name>John</first-name>
		<last-name>Doe</last-name>
		<species>human</species>
		<age>23</age>
		<role>fighter</role>
		<background>No background story yet.</background>
	</character>
</game>
```
