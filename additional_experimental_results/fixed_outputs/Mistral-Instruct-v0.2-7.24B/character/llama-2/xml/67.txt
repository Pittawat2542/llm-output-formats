```vbnet
// Name: Lila Rose
```

## Exercise 6: Create an original story idea with characters and setting

```vbnet
// Title: The Secret Garden
// Main Conflict: A 100-year-old heirloom flower named Mimosa, located in the local botanical garden, is stolen by an unknown thief every year during spring.

// Characters and Setting:
// Zoey Johnson (27), a local florist, passionate about maintaining the gardens' plants and their history. She's been volunteering at Botanica Bloom since she was a teenager.
// Felix Montoya (40), a reclusive neighbor with an expansive greenhouse and a secretive past. He moved to town 5 years ago.
// The Secret Garden, a hidden part of the botanical garden that's been locked for decades. It's said to contain rare and priceless flowers, including the famous Mimosa.

// Key Details:
// Zoey discovers Felix being involved in the annual thefts of Mimosa from Botanica Bloom on a late-night patrol of the garden. She also finds out that he's the thief behind this unsolved case and has been stealing the flower for unknown reasons for years.
// After confronting him, Zoey is forced to make a hard decision - either report her discovery to the police department or protect her friend's secret and keep it quiet. The story explores the conflict between keeping a beloved friend and upholding justice.

// Short Synopsis:
The Secret Garden is a heartfelt tale about Zoey Johnson, a local florist devoted to preserving the history and beauty of the local botanical garden. One day on her late-night patrols, she discovers her neighbor Felix Montoya involved in the annual theft of Mimosa, the 100-year-old heirloom flower cherished by locals. Zoey is torn between reporting this discovery to the police and protecting her longtime friend's secret. As their friendship unravels under the weight of justice, The Secret Garden reveals a story of trust, loyalty, and difficult choices.
```
