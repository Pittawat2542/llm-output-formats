Sure, here is an example of a dialogue in XML format:
```xml
<?xml version="1.0" ?>
<game>
  <dialogues>
    <dialogue>
      <order>1</order>
      <speaker>Player</speaker>
      <text>Hello, I am the player.</text>
    </dialogue>
    <dialogue>
      <order>2</order>
      <speaker>NPC</speaker>
      <text>Hello, I am the non-player character. It is nice to meet you.</text>
    </dialogue>
  </dialogues>
</game>
```
