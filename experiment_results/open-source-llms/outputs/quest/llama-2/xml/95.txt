

### Sample Input
```
id: 1
title: "The Witcher"
objective: "Find the Witcher"
description: "The Witcher is a man who uses his magical powers to fight monsters and other evil beings. He is the only one who can defeat the evil forces that threaten the world."
reward: "2000 gold"
quest-giver: "Geralt of Rivia"
tasks:
	task:
		order: 1
		objective: "Find the Witcher"
		description: "The Witcher is a man who uses his magical powers to fight monsters and other evil beings. He is the only one who can defeat the evil forces that threaten the world."
		location: "The Witcher's house"
```
