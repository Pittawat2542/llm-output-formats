

## Examples

### Example 1

```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<id>1</id>
	<title>The Quest for the Holy Grail</title>
	<objective>Find the Holy Grail</objective>
	<description>The Holy Grail is the cup used by Jesus at the Last Supper. It was later used by Joseph of Arimathea to collect the blood of Jesus as he was being crucified. It is said to have miraculous healing powers. The quest for the Holy Grail is one of the most famous quests in the world.</description>
	<reward>1000 gold pieces</reward>
	<quest-giver>King Arthur</quest-giver>
	<tasks>
		<task>
			<order>1</order>
			<objective>Find the Holy Grail</objective>
			<description>Find the Holy Grail and bring it back to King Arthur.</description>
			<location>Camelot</location>
		</task>
	</tasks>
</game>
```
