

## Example
```xml
<game>
	<id>1234567890</id>
	<title>A Quest for the Ultimate Treasure</title>
	<objective>Find the ultimate treasure</objective>
	<description>A quest to find the ultimate treasure.</description>
	<reward>1000 gold</reward>
	<quest-giver>The king</quest-giver>
	<tasks>
		<task>
			<order>1</order>
			<objective>Find the ultimate treasure</objective>
			<description>Go to the end of the world and get the ultimate treasure.</description>
			<location>The end of the world</location>
		</task>
	</tasks>
</game>
```
