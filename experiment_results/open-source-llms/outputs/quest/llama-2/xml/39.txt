

## Example
```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<id>1</id>
	<title>Quest for the Golden Apple</title>
	<objective>Find the Golden Apple</objective>
	<description>Find the Golden Apple, and bring it to the king. He will reward you with a bag of gold and a magical cloak that will make you invisible.</description>
	<reward>100 gold coins and a magical cloak</reward>
	<quest-giver>King</quest-giver>
	<tasks>
		<task>
			<order>1</order>
			<objective>Find the Golden Apple</objective>
			<description>Find the Golden Apple in the forest</description>
			<location>Forest</location>
		</task>
		<task>
			<order>2</order>
			<objective>Bring the Golden Apple to the king</objective>
			<description>Bring the Golden Apple to the king</description>
			<location>King's palace</location>
		</task>
	</tasks>
</game>
```
