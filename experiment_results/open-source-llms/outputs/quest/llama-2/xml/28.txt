

## Examples

### Example 1
```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<id>1234567890</id>
	<title>The quest of the lost treasure</title>
	<objective>Find the lost treasure</objective>
	<description>You will have to find the lost treasure in the old castle.</description>
	<reward>100 gold</reward>
	<quest-giver>The king</quest-giver>
	<tasks>
		<task>
			<order>1</order>
			<objective>Find the old castle</objective>
			<description>You will have to find the old castle and enter it.</description>
			<location>The old castle</location>
		</task>
		<task>
			<order>2</order>
			<objective>Find the lost treasure</objective>
			<description>You will have to find the lost treasure in the old castle.</description>
			<location>The old castle</location>
		</task>
	</tasks>
</game>
```
