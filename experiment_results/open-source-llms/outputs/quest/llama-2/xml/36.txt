

## Input format

### Example

```markdown
<quest>
	<id>1</id>
	<title>The Quest for the Holy Grail</title>
	<objective>Find the Holy Grail</objective>
	<description>This is a quest to find the Holy Grail. You will be rewarded with the title of "King Arthur".</description>
	<reward>The title of King Arthur</reward>
	<quest-giver>King Arthur</quest-giver>
	<tasks>
		<task>
			<order>1</order>
			<objective>Find the Holy Grail</objective>
			<description>You must find the Holy Grail. The Holy Grail is located in the castle of Camelot.</description>
			<location>Camelot</location>
		</task>
		<task>
			<order>2</order>
			<objective>Find the Holy Grail</objective>
			<description>You must find the Holy Grail. The Holy Grail is located in the castle of Camelot.</description>
			<location>Camelot</location>
		</task>
	</tasks>
</quest>
```

### Required fields

- `id`: The quest ID.
- `title`: The quest title.
- `objective`: The quest objective.
- `description`: The quest description.
- `reward`: The quest reward.
- `quest-giver`: The quest giver.
- `tasks`: The tasks.

### Optional fields

- `order`: The task order.
- `objective`: The task objective.
- `description`: The task description.
- `location`: The task location.
