

### Input

* **id**: a unique identifier for the quest.
* **title**: the title of the quest.
* **objective**: the objective of the quest.
* **description**: the description of the quest.
* **reward**: the reward of the quest.
* **quest-giver**: the quest giver.
* **tasks**: the tasks of the quest.

### Example

```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<id>id</id>
	<title>quest title</title>
	<objective>quest objective</objective>
	<description>quest description</description>
	<reward>quest reward</reward>
	<quest-giver>quest giver</quest-giver>
	<tasks>
		<task>
			<order>task order</order>
			<objective>task objective</objective>
			<description>task description</description>
			<location>task location</location>
		</task>
	</tasks>
</game>
```
