This quest may be difficult for first time players. It should be completed in 6 days, 17 hours, and 32 minutes, and you'll receive 6,900 Gold and 5,600 EXP Quest Points. This is a timed quest. Upon completion, 2,000 Gold will be awarded. The quest must be completed before time runs out in order to receive the reward. Please complete this quest ASAP so you can earn the Gold reward. If you are still having trouble, and have completed the previous quests, you can visit one of the NPC's on the map named [Quest Giver], or a different NPC that has the [Skills] skill in order to receive a little guidance in completing this quest. Please be sure to give credit to [Quest Giver].
