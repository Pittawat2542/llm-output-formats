# Usage
You (the quest giver) are the only one who can complete my quest. If you complete my quest, you will receive an XML reward.
```
<game>
	<id>1234</id>
	<title>Quest Title</title>
	<objective>Complete the quest</objective>
	<description>I will test your mettle. Good luck!</description>
	<reward>XML rewards</reward>
	<quest-giver>Only I can complete it!</quest-giver>
</game>
```
