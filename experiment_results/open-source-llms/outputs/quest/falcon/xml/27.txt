<p><strong><code>Tasks</code></strong></p>
<pre><code>     - The <code>&lt;tasks&gt;</code> tag is used to define the structure of the quest's tasks. <code>objective</code>, <code>description</code>, and <code>location</code> are specific tags within the <code>&lt;tasks&gt;</code> tag that can contain their own sub-task(s) and their respective sub-quest objectives, descriptions, and location.
</code></pre>
<p><strong><code>Title</code></strong></p>
<pre><code>      The <code>&lt;title&gt;</code> tag is used to define the title of the quest.
</code></pre>
<p><strong><code>Giver</code></strong></p>
<pre><code>      The <code>&lt;quest-giver&gt;</code> tag identifies the individual who will be providing information on the quest or completing a particular task for the player.
</code></pre>
