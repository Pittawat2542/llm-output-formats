- **id** | The identifier of this quest.
- **title** | The name of the quest's objective.
- **objective** | The name of the goal or task that must be completed before the quest is completed.
- **quest_giver** | The name of the person or entity giving this quest to the player.
- **quest** | The name of the section/branch of quests under section/branch.
- **quests** + - A list of related quests.
- **\.quest*`.*`** - Filters for the various sections and branches of quests within that category (e.g. `.quest`).
