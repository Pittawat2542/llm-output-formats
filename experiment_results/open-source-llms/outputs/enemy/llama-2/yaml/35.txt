

### Example

```yaml
game:
  enemy:
    description: AI description
    id: 1
    name: AI name
    stats:
      atk: 10
      def: 10
      hp: 10
      mp: 10
      spd: 10
```

### Inputs

- `game`: The game ID
- `enemy`: The enemy ID

### Outputs

- `game`: The game ID
- `enemy`: The enemy ID
- `description`: The description of the enemy
- `id`: The ID of the enemy
- `name`: The name of the enemy
- `stats`: The stats of the enemy
  - `atk`: The attack stat of the enemy
  - `def`: The defense stat of the enemy
  - `hp`: The health stat of the enemy
  - `mp`: The magic stat of the enemy
  - `spd`: The speed stat of the enemy
