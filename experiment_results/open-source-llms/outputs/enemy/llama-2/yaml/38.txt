

### Examples

```yaml
game:
  enemy:
    description: "A large and powerful enemy."
    id: "enemy-1"
    name: "Enemy"
    stats:
      atk: 50
      def: 100
      hp: 100
      mp: 100
      spd: 100
```

### Arguments

- **game**: the game the enemy is in.
- **enemy**: the enemy to generate the status for.

### Options

- **--name**: the name of the enemy.
- **--description**: the description of the enemy.
- **--stats**: the stats of the enemy.
