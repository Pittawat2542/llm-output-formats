

## Inputs

### game

#### enemy

##### id

Required. The id of the enemy.

##### name

Required. The name of the enemy.

##### description

Required. The description of the enemy.

##### stats

Required. The stats of the enemy.

##### hp

Required. The health points of the enemy.

##### mp

Required. The magic points of the enemy.

##### atk

Required. The attack points of the enemy.

##### def

Required. The defense points of the enemy.

##### spd

Required. The speed points of the enemy.
