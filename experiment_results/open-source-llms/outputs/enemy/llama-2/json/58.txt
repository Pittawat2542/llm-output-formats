

### Input

* **game**: The game that the battle is in.

### Output

* **game**: The game that the battle is in.

* **enemy**: The enemy that the battle is against.

* **enemy.id**: The ID of the enemy.

* **enemy.name**: The name of the enemy.

* **enemy.description**: The description of the enemy.

* **enemy.stats**: The stats of the enemy.

* **enemy.stats.hp**: The HP of the enemy.

* **enemy.stats.mp**: The MP of the enemy.

* **enemy.stats.atk**: The attack of the enemy.

* **enemy.stats.def**: The defense of the enemy.

* **enemy.stats.spd**: The speed of the enemy.
