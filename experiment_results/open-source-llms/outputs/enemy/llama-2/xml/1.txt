

## Example
```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<enemy>
		<id>1</id>
		<name>Boss</name>
		<description>The Boss is a powerful enemy with high HP and MP. It has a very high attack and defense.</description>
		<stats>
			<hp>200</hp>
			<mp>100</mp>
			<atk>20</atk>
			<def>20</def>
			<spd>10</spd>
		</stats>
	</enemy>
	<enemy>
		<id>2</id>
		<name>Miniboss</name>
		<description>The Miniboss is a powerful enemy with high HP and MP. It has a very high attack and defense.</description>
		<stats>
			<hp>150</hp>
			<mp>100</mp>
			<atk>15</atk>
			<def>15</def>
			<spd>10</spd>
		</stats>
	</enemy>
</game>
```

## Requirements

* [Markdown](https://www.markdownguide.org/)

## Usage

```shell
$ markdown-to-xml <file>
```

### Example

```shell
$ markdown-to-xml example.md
```
