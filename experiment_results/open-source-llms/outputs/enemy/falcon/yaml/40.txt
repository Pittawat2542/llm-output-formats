# Game variables
- `game.enemy.id`: The id of the enemy to be created.
- `game.enemy.name`: Name of the enemy to be created.
- `game.enemy.stats.atk`: Attack stat of the enemy.
- `game.enemy.stats.def`: Defense stat of the enemy.
- `game.enemy.hp`: Health pemt of the enemy.
- `game.enemy.mp`: Mana pool of the enemy.
- `game.enemy.spd`: Speed stat of the enemy.
