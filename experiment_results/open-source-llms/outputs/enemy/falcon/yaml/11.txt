Explanation:

1. The game section is mandatory and should be the first child of the class.
2. It is possible for more than one enemy to have the same description, name or HP value.
3. `stats()` should be a child of `game` or `enemy`
4. `att(stat)` should be the name of the attack that the enemy has.
5. `def(stat)` should be the name of the defense that the enemy can use against an attack.
6. `hp(stat)` should be a negative integer for HP value or positive for HP value of the enemy.
7. `mp(stat)` should be a value similar to HP, but negative to represent the amount of morale the enemy has.
8. `spd(stat)` should be a positive integer for speed value.
