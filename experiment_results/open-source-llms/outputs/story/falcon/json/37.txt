An elderly video game designer goes on a road trip to try and rediscover his passion for game design. On the way, he finds a talented young designer who has been struggling to make ends meet. As they travel together, they embark on a journey to create the game of their dreams, facing challenges and overcoming obstacles. Along the way, they uncover forgotten stories and old rivalries that have shaped who they are, and learn the true meaning of friendship, courage, and determination.
