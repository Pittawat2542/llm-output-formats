The game is a fantasy RPG set in a mystical land. The player must traverse an immersive world filled with mythical creatures and magical spells as they seek to unravel the dark secrets that have been kept hidden. They will begin their journey by choosing to either defend their homeland against invading barbarians, or by entering a forgotten city to discover its secrets and uncover the hidden truth about their existence. No matter the path the player chooses, they will soon find out that fate has placed them in a dangerous and exciting situation.
