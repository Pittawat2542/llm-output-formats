THE GAME

The world is in chaos. A powerful tyrant has taken over the kingdom, and the people are not happy. The people want a hero to save the kingdom and restore peace and order. It is up to you to step up and become the savior of the realm. Join forces with other heroes, level up your character to defeat your enemies, and go on quests to uncover secrets of power and discover the fate of the kingdom. This is your time to shine, and the ultimate test of your mettle.
