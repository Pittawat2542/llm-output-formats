In a post-apocalyptic world, a lone survivor named Jack must fight for their life against a tyrannical regime in a dilapidated city. Jack must make their way through secret tunnels, abandoned buildings and hostile survivors while they search for the legendary game that could save their city. With their wit, combat skills and a resilient attitude, Jack fights to uncover more information about the game and ultimately take down the regime. Along their journey, they encounter both helpful allies and treacherous enemies who can help or hinder their progress. With every choice, Jack must decide how they want to play their game - and who will be their friend or foe.
