On the night of the annual festival, a group of young magicians performed a powerful spell to bring their missing king back to the throne. The people rejoiced and celebrated with enthusiasm. However, in the midst of the excitement, an evil dark wizard secretly cast a powerful curse on the city to take revenge for the loss of his previous king. Now, it is up to the young magicians to restore peace and restore the throne to the rightful king before the dark wizard's curse takes over the city.
