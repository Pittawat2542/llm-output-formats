A group of astronauts embark on a dangerous mission to retrieve resources from a damaged space station thousands of miles from earth. After many days in space, they finally reach the station and must battle harsh conditions and hostile alien creatures in order to complete their mission. In doing so, they uncover a secret plot to use the station's resources for an alien's nefarious purpose and must use their wits and courage to foil the villain's plans and save the station from destruction.
