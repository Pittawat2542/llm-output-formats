Synopsis:
The game begins at the beginning when a mysterious stranger arrives at a small town to unleash hidden secrets with the power to reshape the world. As players delve through mists of magic and treacherous monsters, they uncover a plot that could destroy the world as it is known. Along the way, the stranger also introduces them to unlikely allies, each with their own hidden agendas. As the players journey further, they become heroes and uncover the key to using their newfound powers to save the world from destruction.
