XML Formatting

1. Indent using spaces or tabs.
2. Add line breaks using the <br> tag.
3. Use "<synopsis>" and "</synopsis>" tags to indicate a synopsis.

Example:
```xml
<xmlns:game="http://example.com/game">
    <game:title>game title</game:title>
    <game:synopsis>game story synopsis</game:synopsis>
    <game:beginning>the beginning of the game</game:beginning>
    <game:ending>the ending of the game</game:ending>
</game>
```
