XML Attributes
- `<title>game title</title>` is required for all games
- `<synopsis>game story synopsis</synopsis>` is required for all games
- `<beginning>title of beginning of the game</beginning>` is required for all games, for example: `the title of the game`
- `<ending>title of ending of the game</ending>` is required for all games, for example: `the end of the game`.
