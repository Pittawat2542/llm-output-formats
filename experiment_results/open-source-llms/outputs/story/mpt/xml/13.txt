Here is an example of the XML of a game story synopsis:
```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<title>The Witness</title>
	<synopsis>A young woman lives with her father in an island that has been cut off from the outside world for decades. One day, she receives a mysterious signal that seems to come from one of the islands that are nearby.</synopsis>
	<beginning>For decades, the island has been cut off from the rest of the world, and its inhabitants are not very friendly with visitors. Our story starts when our main character receives a signal that seems to come from one of the nearby islands.</beginning>
	<ending>She decides to explore one of the islands. What she finds there will change the rest of her life...</ending>
</game>
```
