game story synopsis. The protagonist embarked on an adventure through an exciting and unpredictable land. The main goal of each area were to accumulate a specific amount of gold nuggets. These gold nuggets would purchase much needed equipment, weaponry, and tools, such as armor for protection, stronger swords for defeating enemies, and better shoes to walk across the steep mountains in the middle of the jungle. Upon completing all areas and acquiring all weapons and tools, the protagonist could fight the final boss who was the king of snakes; a serpent with gold teeth and golden eyes who had a treasure of even more gold.
