# game title
The player is a stranger in the kingdom, who just met his wife and two children... 
## game story synopsis
The king of the kingdom saw in his daughter the right person to become his successor. He arranged her marriage with a stranger, and the stranger traveled to become familiar with his new subjects. One evening, while the stranger and his family shared a meal, the king brought his daughter and his eldest son to meet them. It was love at first sight for the princess, who was destined to become the king's successor, with the stranger who would become her future husband. The princess asked her father to grant her new family a house in the kingdom, and to give them a land to farm.
The king went to his court scholar to verify if the stranger could use the magic to make a house appear by wishing
