The heroes are off to rescue the princess! They battle their way through hordes of orcs, trolls, and other nasty creatures, but eventually make it to the castle gates. As they approach, they see that the princess is being carried off in a carriage by the evil knight. The heroes jump into action and begin attacking the castle, but the knight sees them and summons a massive fire-breathing dragon to protect him. The heroes fight their way through the dragon's fiery breath, but the evil knight casts a powerful spell that shrouds the area in a thick fog. The heroes are trapped! can they ever escape?
