Here is the game story synopsis:
The game is set in modern day Washington, D.C. in which protagonist Peter is a young congressional intern who becomes involved in an illicit blackmail plot while working in the Capitol. As the story progresses, Peter finds himself caught up in a web of government fraud, corporate corruption and murder. He must fight to clear his name and uncover the truth behind the scandal, all while trying to navigate the cutthroat world of politics and the secretive world of espionage. 
In the end, Peter finds out that his boss, Russell, is actually responsible for the scandal and blackmail plot. He also learns that Russell's company is responsible for a series of government fraud and lies, which led to the death of Russell's wife. Peter gets shot by Russell in the finale of the story, but is able to escape the scene before he dies.
