Dinsey (game title) is an action-adventure game produced by Squaresoft for the Nintendo DS (game console). The plot follows the plucky hero, Mario, as he explores the magical Kingdom of Lylat, battling against a wide range of alien and robotic foes. During his adventures Mario must also acquire special equipment and fight powerful bosses to advance the plot and save the world from destruction by the evil Bowser!
