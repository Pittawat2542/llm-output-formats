

## Example
```yaml
game:
  beginning: the beginning of the game
  ending: the ending of the game
  synopsis: game story synopsis
  title: game title
```

## Notes

- The beginning and ending of the game are optional.
- The synopsis is optional.
- The title is optional.

## Input

### Required

- `beginning`: The beginning of the game.
- `ending`: The ending of the game.
- `synopsis`: Game story synopsis.
- `title`: Game title.

### Optional

- `beginning`: The beginning of the game.
- `ending`: The ending of the game.
- `synopsis`: Game story synopsis.
- `title`: Game title.
