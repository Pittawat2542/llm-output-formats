

## Input format

### Questions

* What is the name of the game?
* What is the genre of the game?
* What is the story synopsis of the game?
* What is the beginning of the game?
* What is the ending of the game?

### Answers

* The game is called __________.
* The genre of the game is __________.
* The story synopsis of the game is __________.
* The beginning of the game is __________.
* The ending of the game is __________.

### Example

```md
What is the name of the game?
What is the genre of the game?
What is the story synopsis of the game?
What is the beginning of the game?
What is the ending of the game?
```

### Output

```json
{
    "game": {
        "title": "game title",
        "synopsis": "game story synopsis",
        "beginning": "the beginning of the game",
        "ending": "the ending of the game"
    }
}
```
