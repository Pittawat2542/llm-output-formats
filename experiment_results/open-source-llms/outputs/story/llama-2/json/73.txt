

### Sample Input

```
[
    {
        "question": "What is the title of the game?",
        "answer": "game title"
    },
    {
        "question": "What is the synopsis of the game?",
        "answer": "game story synopsis"
    },
    {
        "question": "What is the beginning of the game?",
        "answer": "the beginning of the game"
    },
    {
        "question": "What is the ending of the game?",
        "answer": "the ending of the game"
    }
]
```

### Sample Output

```
{
    "game": {
        "title": "game title",
        "synopsis": "game story synopsis",
        "beginning": "the beginning of the game",
        "ending": "the ending of the game"
    }
}
```

### Explanation

The game title is the first question and the answer is the first element of the input. The game synopsis is the second question and the answer is the second element of the input. The game beginning is the third question and the answer is the third element of the input. The game ending is the fourth question and the answer is the fourth element of the input.

### Hints

- Use recursion to get the synopsis.
- Use recursion to get the beginning and ending of the game.
- Use recursion to get the title of the game.

### Notes

- The game synopsis is the concatenation of the beginning and ending of the game.
- The game title is the concatenation of the beginning and ending of the game.
- The game beginning is the concatenation of the beginning and ending of the game.
- The game ending is the concatenation of the beginning and ending of the game.
