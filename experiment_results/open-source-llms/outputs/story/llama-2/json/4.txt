### Required fields

- **title** - The title of the game
- **synopsis** - The synopsis of the game
- **beginning** - The beginning of the game
- **ending** - The ending of the game

### Optional fields

- **genre** - The genre of the game
- **platform** - The platform of the game
- **publisher** - The publisher of the game
- **developer** - The developer of the game
- **release date** - The release date of the game
- **rating** - The rating of the game
- **cover** - The cover of the game
- **gameplay** - The gameplay of the game
- **mechanics** - The mechanics of the game
- **story** - The story of the game
- **characters** - The characters of the game
- **world** - The world of the game
- **music** - The music of the game
- **other** - Any other information about the game
