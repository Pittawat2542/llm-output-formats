

## Input format

### Text

```
The game is about a boy who wants to be a hero.

The boy is walking through a dark forest when he sees a monster.

The monster is scary and the boy is afraid.

The boy runs away from the monster and finds a castle.

The castle is full of monsters and the boy is scared again.

The boy is running through the castle and he sees a princess.

The princess is locked in a tower and the boy wants to save her.

The boy climbs the tower and he sees the princess.

The princess is beautiful and the boy wants to kiss her.

The boy kisses the princess and he falls in love with her.

The boy and the princess get married and they live happily ever after.
```
