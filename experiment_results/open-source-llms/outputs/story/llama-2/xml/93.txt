

## Input format

### General

You must provide the following information:

- [ ] Title
- [ ] Synopsis
- [ ] Beginning
- [ ] Ending

### Title

The title must be in the format of `game title`.

### Synopsis

The synopsis must be in the format of `game story synopsis`.

### Beginning

The beginning must be in the format of `the beginning of the game`.

### Ending

The ending must be in the format of `the ending of the game`.

### Example

```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<title>game title</title>
	<synopsis>game story synopsis</synopsis>
	<beginning>the beginning of the game</beginning>
	<ending>the ending of the game</ending>
</game>
```

### Rules

- You must provide all the required information.
- You must not provide any other information.
