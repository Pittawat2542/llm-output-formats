

# Input format

## Game title

The game title is a short title of the game. It should be no more than 20 characters long.

## Game synopsis

The game synopsis is a short story of the game. It should be no more than 100 words long.

## Game beginning

The game beginning is the beginning of the game. It should be no more than 100 words long.

## Game ending

The game ending is the ending of the game. It should be no more than 100 words long.
