The player character is a male Orc from the southern barony called Ironfang. They are twenty-eight years old and are a bard. The character was born in the local Orc camp to a father called Wreaker and an unknown mother.

They have fair skin for an Orc, brown hair, grey eyes and height of five feet seven inches. Their strength and dexterity are both above eight, while intelligence and constitution are both four. They are very well known in the area because of their singing voice and are friends with the halfling Clover.
