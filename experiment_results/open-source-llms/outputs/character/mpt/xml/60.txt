A person can be represented as an instance of the class character. This includes a primary identifier, or “id”, the full name of the character, along with more specific identifiers for the character’s kind, such as “species” and “role”. Additional attributes include their age, and an explanation of the type of character. Additional attributes include the story-related background of the character and the location from which they are from. Attributes describing the physical appearance of the character include a description of their eye color, hair color, height, and weight.
