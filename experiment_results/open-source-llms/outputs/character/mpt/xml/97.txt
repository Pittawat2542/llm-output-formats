## Characters:
- id: The unique ID of the character; this can be randomized or pulled from a database
- first-name: The first name of the character
- last-name: The last name of the character
- species: The species of the character, if any
- age: Exact age of the character or a description of the character's age; for example, "20 years-old"
- role: The overall role that the character is playing in the game, like player or NPC
- background: A short background story or history for the character
- place-of-birth: The location where the character was born
- physical-appearance:
  - eye-color: The color of the character's eyes
  - hair-color: The color of the character's hair
  - height: The height of the character in meters
  - weight: The weight of the character in kilograms
