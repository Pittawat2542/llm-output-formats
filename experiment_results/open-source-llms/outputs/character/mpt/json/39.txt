### Character: Robin
### Place of birth: East Blue
### Role: Straw Hat Pirate
### Background: An orphan who survived a brutal plane crash that killed his parents, Robin was taken in by a group of pirates, where he lived with them for two years before realizing their criminal nature. When attempting to get away from them, the pirates set ablaze the village he was hiding in. He was saved by Luffy, Nami, and Usopp, who took him to Ace. He has served as Ace's apprentice ever since and is the master of the Straw Hat Pirates.
