> # Characters
> 
> #### Character #1
> 
> Character #1, also known as 'Snape', was born in Hogwarts to Lily and James Potter. He was a member of the Gryffindor House and was well know for his potion skills. 
> 
> One piece of information to help you understand Snape better is his species which is Hufflepuff. He is also of mixed blood, being half-blood himself.
> 
> "Snape" was also in Dumbledore's Army for a short period where was given a potion that could have killed him. 
> 
> Finally to round out his character, Snape was a member of the "Triple Challenge".
