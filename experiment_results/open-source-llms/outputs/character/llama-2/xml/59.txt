

# Input format
```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<character>
		<id>id</id>
		<first-name>first name</first-name>
		<last-name>last name</last-name>
		<species>species</species>
		<age>exact age or description</age>
		<role>role of the character</role>
		<background>background story</background>
		<place-of-birth>location</place-of-birth>
		<physical-appearance>
			<physical-appearance>
				<eye-color>eye color</eye-color>
				<hair-color>hair color</hair-color>
				<height>height in float value</height>
				<weight>weight in float value</weight>
			</physical-appearance>
		</physical-appearance>
	</character>
</game>
``` [/INST]

# Example
```xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<game>
	<character>
		<id>1</id>
		<first-name>John</first-name>
		<last-name>Smith</last-name>
		<species>human</species>
		<age>32</age>
		<role>doctor</role>
		<background>John Smith was born in 1987. He is a doctor. He lives in London. He has brown eyes and brown hair. He is 6'4" and 200 lbs.</background>
		<place-of-birth>London</place-of-birth>
		<physical-appearance>
			<physical-appearance>
				<eye-color>brown</eye-color>
				<hair-color>brown</hair-color>
				<height>6'4"</height>
				<weight>200 lbs</weight>
			</physical-appearance>
		</physical-appearance>
	</character>
</game>
``` [/INST]
