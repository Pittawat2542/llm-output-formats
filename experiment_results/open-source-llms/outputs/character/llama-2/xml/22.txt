		<role>Doctor</role>

		<place-of-birth>London, England</place-of-birth>
		<physical-appearance>
			<physical-appearance>
				<eye-color>Brown</eye-color>
				<hair-color>Black</hair-color>
				<height>175cm</height>
				<weight>80kg</weight>
			</physical-appearance>
		</physical-appearance>
	</character>
	<character>
		<id>2</id>
		<first-name>Mary</first-name>
		<last-name>Jones</last-name>
		<species>Human</species>
		<age>23</age>
		<role>Nurse</role>
		<background>Mary was born in London, England. She was raised by her parents. She attended medical school in London. She worked as a nurse in London for 5 years. She moved to New York City in 2012 to work as a nurse. She has been working as a nurse in New York City for 5 years.</background>

			<physical-appearance>

			</physical-appearance>
		</physical-appearance>
	</character>
</game>
```
