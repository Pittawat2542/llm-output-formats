

## Input format

### Character ID

The character ID is a unique identifier for the character. It is used to identify the character in the game.

### First name

The first name of the character.

### Last name

The last name of the character.

### Species

The species of the character.

### Age

The age of the character.

### Role

The role of the character.

### Background

The background of the character.

### Place of birth

The place of birth of the character.

### Physical appearance

The physical appearance of the character.

### Eye color

The eye color of the character.

### Hair color

The hair color of the character.

### Height

The height of the character.

### Weight

The weight of the character.
