

# Example
```yaml
game:
  character:
    age: 22
    background: "I was born on the streets of New York City and raised by a loving family"
    first_name: "John"
    id: 1
    last_name: "Smith"
    physical_appearance:
    - eye_color: brown
      hair_color: brown
      height: 6'0
      weight: 180
    place_of_birth: New York City
    role: hero
    species: human
```
