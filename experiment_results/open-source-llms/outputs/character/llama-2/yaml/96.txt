

## Examples

```yaml
game:
  character:
    age: 21
    background: The character was born in a small town in the United States.
    first_name: John
    id: 21
    last_name: Smith
    physical_appearance:
    - eye_color: brown
      hair_color: black
      height: 5'11"
      weight: 180 lbs
    place_of_birth: United States
    role: The character is a police officer.
    species: human
```

## Notes

The output format is the same as the [YAML](https://yaml.org/) format.
