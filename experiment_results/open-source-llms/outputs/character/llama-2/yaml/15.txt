

## Example
```yaml
game:
  character:
    age: 22
    background: a background story
    first_name: first name
    id: id
    last_name: last name
    physical_appearance:
    - eye_color: blue
      hair_color: brown
      height: 5'5"
      weight: 120 lbs
    place_of_birth: a location
    role: a role of the character
    species: human
```
