

## Example
```yaml
game:
  character:
    age: 35
    background: A former soldier who has lost his way.
    first_name: John
    id: 1234
    last_name: Smith
    physical_appearance:
    - eye_color: blue
      hair_color: brown
      height: 170
      weight: 70
    place_of_birth: New York
    role: The main character
    species: Human
```

## Constraints
- The character's age must be an exact number.
- The character's background must be a sentence.
- The character's first name must be a sentence.
- The character's last name must be a sentence.
- The character's physical appearance must be an array of objects.
- The character's physical appearance objects must have the following properties:
  - eye_color: the color of the character's eyes
  - hair_color: the color of the character's hair
  - height: the character's height in feet and inches
  - weight: the character's weight in pounds
- The character's place of birth must be a sentence.
- The character's role must be a sentence.
- The character's species must be a sentence.
