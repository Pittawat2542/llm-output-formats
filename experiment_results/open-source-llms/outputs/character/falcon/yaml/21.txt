Character Name: Mark Johnson

Mark Johnson is a 20-year-old black man standing tall at 5'10. He has a muscular build and a stoic facial expression typical of his African American roots. Though he has grown up in an era of integration, he often feels like an outsider and is eager to make his mark on the world. His family has a tradition of serving as protectors and guardians of their community, and he feels it is his calling to do the same. Mark graduated high school early and is currently studying medical sciences with an eye towards becoming a surgeon or medical practitioner.
