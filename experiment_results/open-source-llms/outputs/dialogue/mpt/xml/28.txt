# Dialogue

"It's over!"

"You have no idea, little one! The war is only starting for me!"

"Oh,"

"There is nothing to fear! Not even death. I will never die, I will live on forever! You cannot defeat me!"

"I don't want to fight."

"That is not an acceptable answer!"

"Fine, I won't let you live, either."

"You won't get far in this galaxy, little one! I am powerful, beyond imagination! You are weak, foolish, and will not get far at all!"

"Well, I guess we will have to fight."
