The sun shone down, the grass swayed softly to the gentle wind, and the birds sang sweetly with the awakening of the day.

I walked over to the window and opened the blinds to let in the warm morning light that streamed in through the newly opening branches. I took a deep breath and let the refreshing morning breeze caress my face.

As my eyes fell upon the peaceful scene before me I was drawn into a moment of reflective silence. The stillness was so calming and I longed for a day of peace and tranquility.

Suddenly the familiar sound of a car engine breaking idle interrupted my reverie. I looked down the street to see a police car pulling up to a local restaurant. I didn't need to look up to tell you that the car was full of police officers!

And there came another car that joined the police car. And more and more cars came. All filled with police officers...
